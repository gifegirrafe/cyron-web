Payment rules & request guide

Understand how Cyron payouts work.

Learn the basic payment rules, what makes a submission eligible, and the correct steps to request your creator payout.

Payment Rules

1

Only approved videos count

A video must be approved by admin before it becomes eligible for payout.

2

Views must be verified

YouTube views are synced using the submitted public video link. Private, deleted, or restricted videos may not count.

3

Maximum cap per video

Each approved video can earn up to $150 unless admin manually decides otherwise.

4

No duplicate payout requests

Once a video is requested for payout, it cannot be selected again for another request.

5

Admin review is required

Every payout request starts as Pending and can be marked Approved, Rejected, or Paid by admin.

How to Request Payment

1

Submit your campaign content

Go to the Campaigns page and submit your public YouTube Shorts or content link.

2

Wait for admin approval

Your content will show as Under Process until admin approves or rejects it.

3

Sync views in dashboard

Use the Dashboard to sync YouTube views and calculate estimated earnings.

4

Open the Payments page

Approved videos that are not already requested will appear in the payout selection list.

5

Choose method and submit

Select PayPal, USDT TRC20, or Robux, enter your payment address, and submit the request.

6

Track status in My Payouts

Go to My Payouts to see whether your request is Pending, Approved, Rejected, or Paid.

Important reminder

Cyron saves creator accounts, submissions, approvals, and payout requests using Firebase. This helps payment data stay available across supported devices and sessions.
